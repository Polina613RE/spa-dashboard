How to export from Salesforce
1
SPA Report
Open the SPA Report in Salesforce → click the export/download button → select CSV format → save to your computer.
2
Commission Report
Open the Commission Report in Salesforce → export as CSV. Make sure it includes Fee Amount (col M) and Commission Payment (col O).
3
Upload both above
Drop both files into the upload zones above, then click "Process & build dashboard". All columns calculate automatically.
