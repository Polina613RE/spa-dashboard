How to export from Salesforce
1
SPA Report
Open the SPA Report in Salesforce → click the export/download button → select CSV format → save to your computer.
2
Commission Report
Open the Commission Report in Salesforce → export as CSV. Make sure it includes Fee Amount (col M) and Commission Payment (col O).
3
Upload both above
Drop both files into the upload zones above, then click "Process & build dashboard". All columns calculate automatically.

Reconciliation
Compare your Syndicator-Summary calc against Salesforce Remittances Paid
ℹ️ This compares two numbers per syndicator/month: your calculated Syn Inc Offset vs Salesforce's Gross Amount, and your calculated Mgmt Fee vs Salesforce's Total Mgmt Fees. Differences are shown exactly — nothing is hidden or rounded away. Small gaps that disappear in the Cumulative view are almost always timing (remittance landed a few days into the next month); gaps that persist in the cumulative view are real discrepancies worth investigating.
